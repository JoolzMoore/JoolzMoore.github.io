Self confidence


Date: 27 April Updated: 26 June 2016

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?
Finding and using accurate self-assessment, so y ou have nothing to hide from yourself. Using your own reflections of your experiences and knowing your strengths and weaknesses so you are more comfortable in your own skin. Shifting the attention of your emotions and modulating them, so you are processing the view of yourself without emotions hijacking your experiences. Choosing your experiences without compulsion, and maintaining self -reflectiveness even in the midst of turbulent emotions.

My self awareness practice I started journalling. lets see if I can keep it up, the two minutes thinking and reflecting went so fast. I've always kept copius notes, and done some sketching, but this is more personal. I have done life plans etc, but this will help me actually help evaluate what and how I am learning each day.

What I took away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section
We are not our emotions, and not to let them carry you away like a runaway horse. Feel and understand the emotions, know your internal state and be comfortable with yourself to be able to process emotions, and be clear about who you are and what you want to be.
