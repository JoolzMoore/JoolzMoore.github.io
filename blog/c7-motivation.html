Motivation


Date: 3 July 2016

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?
There are three types of happiness: pleasure, passion, and higher purpose:
> Pleasure: Chasing the next high
> Passion: In the "flow" with peak performance which meets peak engagement.
> Higher purpose: Finding and responding to something bigger than you

After reading the 'discovering values and higher purpose,' my core values, and what I stand for: Flexibility, Consistency, Creativity, Challenge, Making a difference, Equality, Education, Diversity, Sustainability, Freedom

Motivation comes from:
> Alignment: Defining values
> Envisioning: Knowing the desired future
> Resilience: Overcoming obstacles

The ultimate motivator:
> Autonomy: Be involved in our work direction
> Mastery: Getting better all the time at something that matters
> Purpose: Doing something larger than ourselves

Starting from today, if everything meets or exceeds my most optimistic expectations, what will my life be in five years: Being healthier and keeping fit so I am there for my family and can create something useful to help us communicate and look after ourselves and our world...
