Self confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?
Shifting the attention of your emotions and modulating them, so you are processing the view of yourself without emotions hijacking your experiences. Choosing your experiwnces withour compulsion.

Self awareness practice
One good practise is to use the body scan to understand feelings as they arise. Accept emotions, and be wiling to experience them with with curiosity and humour. Imagining yourself as porous so the emotions go right through you without harm. Watch them come and go. Choose health and freedom over fear, as fear is not being in control of your emotions.

Take-aways: Shifting the attention from the power of emotion. We can use the clarity of our values instead of our feelings. W do not want to be like the example of the person riding the runaway horse. The person has no mastery over their emotions and where they want to go. They let the horse choose and have to hold on, and follow by compulsion, not by freedom of their choice.
