Daily mindfulness

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?
II often mention to others to have a go and that the process is everything, but can I adhere to my own advice? I try to do everything as best I can, as outcomes ae important. And as a mum, so many plans are made, but not fulfilled, life happens! Other people have other plans... I have to remember this course is for me and I should be like the children at preschool - getting hands all sticky and gooey and covered in the experience of just learning. Enjoy it, at whatever pace, I have to be happy with what I can get done in the time I currently have available. It is about experimenting and learning with everything new.

Practising mindful conversation
Listen, listen. Murmer a positive response. It shows that you have listened, and then listen some more. Add in some humanity, valuing others thoughts and lavish on your empathy - the world sure needs a whole lot more. Enjoy the conversation, you will remember it all the more. Your thoughts were concentrated, and not wandering elsewhere, and you were willing to be part of sharing.
